What is flaming

Example 1: Social Media Argument

User1: "I think this new policy is a great idea."
User2: "Are you kidding? It's the dumbest thing I've ever heard. Only someone with no clue would support it."

Example 2: Celebrity Fan Feud

User1: "I love Actor X's performances!"
User2: "Anyone who admires Actor X is tasteless. Their acting is terrible, and you must be blind not to see it."

Example 3: Forum Debate

User1: "I prefer this game's visual effects over the other one."
User2: "You must be delusional. The one you like is garbage. stop spewing nonsense."
