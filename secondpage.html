What is flaming


Example 1: Insult

User 1: "I think this new policy is a great idea."
User 2: "Are you kidding? It's the dumbest thing I've ever heard. Only someone with no clue would support it."

Example 2: CAPSLOCK WARRIOR

User 1: "I love Actor X's performances!"
User 2: "ANYONE WHO ADMIRES ACTOR X IS TASTELESS."

Example 3: Troll

User 1: "Look at my new puppy that I just adopted today!"
User 2: "Wow, your puppy looks like it's already on the brink of obesity. You should probably put it on a diet before it becomes the next Guinness World Record for the fattest dog. Just saying..."

Causes of internet flaming


Anonymity

online flamers can hide their identity by creating multiple fake accounts.


Miscommunications

netizens may misunderstood other netizens intentions and meaning, leading to conflicts


Echo Chambers

netizens spreading false information and rumours and other netizens believe it and continue spreading these news.

How To Prevent online flaming

  • Fact checking: magnifying glass checking facts, ensure what you see online is true by cross-checking with official websites like CNA

  • Positive Engagement:communicate with other netizens with respect and prevent starting unnecessary conflicts

  • report: report to relevant authorities when you encounter online flaming
